Who made CaregiverNet?

March 14, 2016 | Camille Benson, Julia Bindler, Jonathan Brodie, Stephen Grinich, Sam Hinh, and Risako Owan

  • Camille: Camille is our Director of Information Technology. She organizes events by sending us GroupMe messages. Her hobbies include hunting dinosaurs and making salt-water taffy
  • Julia: Infrastructure Liaison. “No one really knows what she does nor do we know what this title means” - Camile. Julia enjoys studying tarantulas and sea stars in her free time.
  • Jonathan: Jonathan is our lead graphic designer, but only worked on the backend architecture. “We wanted to place him in a place where he is most comfortable. His skills needed diversifying” - Stephen
  • Stephen: Stephen is our Global Marketing Liason. He is fluent in English, Latin, and that one language in Avatar. He mainly works off shore in Toronto where is is also studying criminology.
  • Sam: Sam is the Chief Financial Officer of CaregiverNet. He manages our $15 budget to attend one local convention. He is an essential part of the team when it come to money.
  • Risako: Risako is our trusted CEO. After buying all of our shares she has full control of the company. Her leadership style is like a mixture between Gandalf, Dumbledore, and Iron Man.
